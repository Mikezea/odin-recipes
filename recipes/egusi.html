Egusi soup

image of a finished egusi soup

This is a very delicious food popularly known as egusi soup. it is one of the Afrcan best delicasy

It is better enjoyed with eba or akpu. But in the absence of akpu or eba, egusi soup can as well be enjoyed with semovita.
Wishing you a happy eating of our local delicasy

ingredence needed to cook egusi soup

  • water
  • melon
  • vegetables
  • salt
  • okpeye
  • kpomo
  • dry fish

cooking steps

  1. Add water to your port and boil
  2. At 100 deg. add oil
  3. Mix melon with water and add
  4. Put salt and peper
  5. put you kpomo and dried fish
  6. Allow it to done
